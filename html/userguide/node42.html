Image Coordinate Systems

Miriad correctly handles a variety of image coordinate systems (the mapping between pixel and physical coordinates) in a correct fashion. The coordinates (and their description within the dataset) are treated in a fashion similar to AIPS (see Eric Greisen's AIPS memo No. 27, ``Non-linear coordinate systems in AIPS'').
