FFT Routines

There are three FFT routines, namely: 
      subroutine fftrc(in,out,sign,n)
      subroutine fftcr(in,out,sign,n)
      subroutine fftcc(in,out,sign,n)
These perform one-dimensional FFTs. In all cases, sign is the sign of the exponent in the transform (i.e. a sign of -1 is conventionally viewed as a forward transform), and n is a power of 2 giving the length of the (full) sequence. scaling is never performed (it is up to you to scale at the best time).
